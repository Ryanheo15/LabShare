Molecular Mass Spectrometry Facility

Location: Urey Hall Rooms 2237-2261, Revelle College Plaza, Ridge Walk

  • Electrospray ionization (ESI) and atmospheric pressure chemical ionization mass spectrometry (ESI/APCI-MS) analysis for both small organic molecules and biomolecules.
  • High performance liquid chromatograph ESI/APCI-MS (LC-ESI/APCI-MS) and LC-ESI/APCI-MS/MS analysis with simultaneous UV-VIS detection.
  • Gas chromatographic mass spectrometry (GC-MS) analysis with electron impact source.
  • High mass resolution and high mass accuracy (±5 parts per million, ppm) analysis of small organic molecules, synthetic peptides, and organometallic compounds by chemical ionization, and electrospray ionization.
  • Assistance in experimental design with respect to MS analysis and/or HPLC method development.

Hours: 9:00am - 5:00pm
